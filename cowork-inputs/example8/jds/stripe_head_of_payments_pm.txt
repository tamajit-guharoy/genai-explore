JOB DESCRIPTION

Role: Head of Product — Payments Infrastructure
Company: Stripe
Location: London, UK (Hybrid — 3 days/week in office)
Team: Payments Platform
Reports to: VP Product, Payments

---

ABOUT STRIPE
Stripe is a financial infrastructure platform for businesses. Millions of companies—from the world's largest
enterprises to the most ambitious startups—use Stripe to accept payments, grow their revenue, and accelerate
new business opportunities. Our mission is to grow the GDP of the internet.

---

THE ROLE
We're looking for an experienced product leader to own the strategy and execution of Stripe's payments
infrastructure products — the underlying rails, routing logic, and reliability systems that process
hundreds of billions of dollars annually.

This is a high-visibility, high-impact role. You'll work directly with our largest enterprise customers,
shape multi-year infrastructure roadmaps, and lead a team of senior PMs.

---

WHAT YOU'LL DO
- Define and own the product vision for Stripe's core payment processing infrastructure
- Lead a team of 4 Senior PMs and 2 PMs; set growth frameworks and manage performance
- Partner with Engineering, Risk, and Data Science to ship high-reliability payment systems
- Own reliability metrics: payment success rates, latency, uptime — across 135+ currencies and 50+ methods
- Drive enterprise customer conversations to understand infrastructure pain points and turn them into product
- Represent Stripe externally at industry events and in regulatory discussions (FCA, PSD3)
- Work across Stripe's global infrastructure to improve payment routing and optimisation

---

WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR
Required:
- 7+ years product management experience; 3+ years leading PM teams
- Deep expertise in payments infrastructure (card networks, bank rails, instant payments, global methods)
- Experience with high-scale distributed systems (understanding rather than building)
- Track record of shipping products at significant transaction volume (£1B+/year preferred)
- Outstanding cross-functional stakeholder management and communication

Preferred:
- Experience with Open Banking, PSD2/PSD3, or real-time payment schemes (FPS, SEPA Inst, RTP)
- Familiarity with fraud, risk, and compliance in payments
- Experience working with enterprise customers at C-suite level
- Regulatory/policy experience with central banks or payment schemes

---

STRIPE'S VALUES
We believe that the economy should be more accessible, and that starting and scaling a business should be
as easy as possible. We work to be thorough, curious, and direct — and we value those qualities in our team.

---

COMPENSATION
Competitive base salary + equity + benefits. Band: £150,000–£185,000 base (depending on experience).
