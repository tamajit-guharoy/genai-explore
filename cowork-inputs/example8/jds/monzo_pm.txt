JOB DESCRIPTION

Role: Senior Product Manager — Lending
Company: Monzo
Location: London, UK (Hybrid — 2 days/week)
Team: Consumer Credit
Reports to: Director of Product, Credit

---

ABOUT MONZO
Monzo is a UK digital bank with over 9 million customers. We're building the best bank in the world —
one that people genuinely love and that improves their financial lives. We're profitable, growing fast,
and working toward a full-service banking licence in the US.

---

THE ROLE
We need a Senior PM to join our Lending team. You'll own product strategy for personal loans and
overdrafts — products that are already used by over 1.5 million Monzo customers.

This is a deeply cross-functional role. You'll work with credit risk, data science, compliance,
and operations to build lending products that are genuinely good for customers.

---

WHAT YOU'LL OWN
- Product roadmap for personal loans (£1K–£25K, 1–5 year terms) and arranged overdrafts
- Credit decisioning strategy — partnering with risk team to improve approval rates while managing loss
- Customer experience across the loan lifecycle: apply → approve → disburse → manage → close
- Consumer Duty compliance for all lending products (you'll own the evidence framework)
- Responsible lending principles — this matters deeply at Monzo

---

WHAT WE NEED
Essential:
- 5+ years PM experience; lending or credit product experience preferred
- Strong understanding of credit risk fundamentals (LTV, PD, LGD, ECL)
- Familiarity with FCA Consumer Duty and responsible lending regulation
- Proven ability to balance commercial outcomes with customer outcomes
- Experience with data-heavy, compliance-sensitive product environments

Desirable:
- Open Banking data in credit decisioning
- Affordability assessment tools
- BNPL or embedded credit experience

---

MONZO VALUES
We care deeply about doing the right thing by customers — especially in lending, where the stakes
are high. We're looking for someone who shares that conviction and can navigate regulatory complexity
without losing sight of what customers actually need.

---

COMPENSATION
£95,000–£120,000 + equity + generous benefits (pension, healthcare, learning budget)
