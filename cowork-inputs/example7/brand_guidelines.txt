ABOUT RETAILCO
RetailCo is a UK retail chain operating 200 stores across England, Scotland, and Wales.
Founded 1987. Headquartered in Manchester.
Tagline: "Quality for every day."
Core values: Accessibility, Reliability, Community.

---

TONE OF VOICE
- Clear and direct. No jargon, no buzzwords.
- Warm but professional. We talk to customers, not at them.
- Use active voice. "We deliver" not "Deliveries are made by us."
- Plain English. If a Year 10 student can't understand it, rewrite it.

WORDS WE USE: simple, honest, straightforward, value, community, everyday
WORDS WE AVOID: leverage, synergies, best-in-class, world-class, holistic, utilise

DOCUMENT FORMATTING

Reports:
  - Cover page with RetailCo logo, document title, date, and "CONFIDENTIAL" watermark
  - Executive summary on page 1 (max 200 words)
  - Section headings in RetailCo Red (#E63946)
  - Page numbers in footer: right-aligned
  - Footer text: "RetailCo Ltd | Confidential | [Date]"

Presentations:
  - Use RetailCo PowerPoint template (on SharePoint)
  - Title slide: white text on RetailCo Red background
  - Body slides: white background, RetailCo Red accents
  - Maximum 6 bullet points per slide; prefer visuals over text
