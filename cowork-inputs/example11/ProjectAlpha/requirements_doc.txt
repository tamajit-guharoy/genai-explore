PROJECT ALPHA — PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT
Version: 2.3 | Last updated: 28 May 2026 | Owner: Priya Nair (CPO)

---

PROJECT OVERVIEW
Project Alpha is the codename for the enterprise analytics dashboard — our most-requested enterprise feature.
Target launch: end of Q3 2026 (30 September).

---

PROBLEM STATEMENT
Enterprise customers (>100 seats) have no consolidated view of their usage, team activity,
and ROI metrics within the platform. Currently they extract data manually via API or request
reports from our Customer Success team. This creates churn risk and limits expansion.

---

GOALS
1. Reduce manual reporting requests to CS team by 80%
2. Increase enterprise NPS by 10 points (from 42 to 52)
3. Reduce enterprise churn from 3.2% to 2.0% within 2 quarters of launch

---

SCOPE — IN

Feature 1: Usage Overview Dashboard
- Total active users (daily/weekly/monthly) across all account seats
- Feature adoption heatmap (which features are being used vs not)
- Seat utilisation: % of paid seats with activity in last 30 days

Feature 2: Team Activity Reports
- Exportable activity report by team or department (requires admin hierarchy)
- Comparison view: this period vs last period
- Scheduled email delivery of reports (weekly/monthly)

Feature 3: ROI Metrics Panel
- Time-saved calculator (user inputs value per hour; platform shows estimated hours saved)
- Document output tracker (total docs created, edited, exported)
- API usage breakdown

Feature 4: Admin Controls
- Seat management: reassign, deactivate, invite
- SSO usage report
- Permission audit log

---

SCOPE — OUT (deferred to Phase 2)
- Real-time activity streaming
- Custom report builder with query interface
- Multi-account / parent-child account views
- Integration with external BI tools (Tableau, Looker)

---

TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS
- Dashboard must load in under 3 seconds for accounts with up to 2,000 users
- Data freshness: within 24 hours of activity
- Export formats: CSV, PDF, Excel
- RBAC: only account admins can access; view-only for team managers (scoped to their team)
- GDPR: must not expose individual user activity to non-admin roles

---

MILESTONES
Week 1-2 (currently): Technical design review and stakeholder sign-off
Week 3-4: Feature 1 build begins
Week 6: Feature 1 beta with 5 enterprise customers
Week 8: Features 1 and 2 complete
Week 10: Features 3 and 4 complete
Week 11-12: QA, security review, beta expansion
Week 13: General availability launch
